University of Pittsburgh Social Media Campaign

Together, let's make sure Pitt's brand flows across all social media platforms and makes the school as desireable as possible.

Basically...

Social media reigns supreme in today's society, as most teenagers and young adults have Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, etc.

Reasons why this project matters...

...and why you should want to be a part of this team

  1. This project will make it easier for both applicants and students to search through Pitt's social media archives.
  2. Pitt is known for being one step ahead of the game... what other school has an outlet where all of their social media is featured in one place?
  3. Social media is trending right now, and I do not see this trend going anywhere for a long time.

So, what elements am I thinking of including?

To start, this project needs to bring together information from most- if not all- of Pitt's social media.

This includes a section dedicated to the most popular forms, including YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter.

...We can even compose our own images and sound to include in a different section!

The main focus would be on the most recent posts, therefore everyone can easily stay up to date with the happenings at Pitt on a daily basis.

In this way, we can guarantee Pitt's fairly new re-branding works well with a select target audience.

Sometimes, you need to bring things to other people's attention to get them to actually pay attention
